Sales value (in lakhs)

SALES VALUE CHART:
This chart plots the graph for the total sales value (in lakhs) that happened for every month of the year. Additionally, it also plots the same graph for the previous year as well. This enables us to analyze and formulate business strategies based on the sales output for each month.

TIP #1: Hover your mouse cursor over the graph to see and compare the datasets for each month between the two years.

TIP #2: Click on the 2018 or 2019 box (index key) in the chart to disable the datasets of a specific year so as to focus on just one graph.
